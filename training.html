Grand Traverse — Training Principles (UA Method)

01
Vert is the metric. In ski season, track feet climbed — not miles. Distance lies on snow.
02
Build vert progressively. Target 15–20k ft/month by December · 20–25k ft/month by January.
03
GT is a Z1/Z2 race. Aerobic base is everything. The fastest teams move easy for hours.
04
Skin technique = free speed. Efficiency matters as much as fitness. Practice on every outing.
05
Rehearse the night. 2–3 night ski tours minimum before race day. Gear, lighting, fueling.
06
Partner is a system. Identify by fall 2026. Train together. Pace, communication, gear redundancy.
